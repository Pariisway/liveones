Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I earn as a Whisper?

Whispers earn $12 for every 5-minute call. Your earnings depend on your availability and how many calls you accept. Full-time Whispers can earn $2,000-$4,000 per month.

When and how do I get paid?

Payments are processed bi-weekly via your chosen payment method (PayPal, bank transfer, etc.). There's a minimum payout threshold of $50. All payments include detailed earnings statements.

What equipment do I need?

You'll need a computer or smartphone with a stable internet connection, a quality headset or microphone for clear audio, and a webcam for video calls. A quiet, private space is also required.

Can I choose when to be available?

Yes! You have complete control over your schedule. You can turn your availability on/off anytime through your dashboard. Many Whispers work part-time during evenings and weekends.

What topics can I discuss?

You can discuss a wide range of topics within our community guidelines. Popular categories include entertainment, gaming, music, sports, career advice, lifestyle, and general conversation.

What happens if I have a technical issue during a call?

Our platform automatically detects connection issues. If a call drops due to technical problems, we have systems in place to handle refunds and protect your earnings.

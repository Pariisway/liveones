Common Questions

Before contacting us, you might find your answer in our FAQ.

How do I become a Whisper?

Visit the "Become a Whisper" page to apply. You'll need to submit an application with your details and verification documents.

How much does a call cost?

Each 5-minute call costs 1 Whisper Coin. You can purchase coins in packages of 5, 10, 20, or 50 with volume discounts.

When do Whispers get paid?

Whispers receive payments bi-weekly via their chosen payment method, with a minimum payout threshold of $50.

How do I report inappropriate behavior?

Use the "Report" button during calls, report from your call history, or email support@whisperchat.com with details.

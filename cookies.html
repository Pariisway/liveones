This Cookie Policy explains how Whisper+Me uses cookies and similar technologies on our platform.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

Cookies may be either "persistent" cookies or "session" cookies:

  • Persistent cookies: Remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them
  • Session cookies: Temporary and deleted when you close your browser

We also use similar technologies such as:

  • Local Storage: Stores data locally in your browser
  • Session Storage: Temporary storage for your browsing session
  • Pixels/Tags: Tiny images that help us understand user behavior

2. How We Use Cookies

Cookie Type Purpose Examples
Necessary Essential for website functionality Authentication, security, session management
Functional Remember your preferences Language settings, display preferences
Analytics Understand how users interact Google Analytics, page views, user flows
Advertising Show relevant advertisements Google AdSense, retargeting campaigns

Necessary cookies are essential and cannot be turned off. They are usually only set in response to actions you take, such as logging in or filling in forms.

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your device and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed.

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to manage cookie preferences:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Opt-Out Tools

Use these tools to opt-out of tracking:

  • Google Analytics Opt-out
  • Network Advertising Initiative
  • Your Online Choices

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about WhisperChat. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our support team.

What is WhisperChat?

WhisperChat is a platform that connects users for meaningful voice and video conversations. Users ("Callers") can connect with verified individuals ("Whispers") for 5-minute paid conversations on various topics including entertainment, gaming, lifestyle, and more.

Is WhisperChat safe to use?

Yes, we prioritize user safety through:

  • User verification and screening
  • Community guidelines enforcement
  • Reporting and moderation systems
  • Secure payment processing
  • No personal information sharing during calls

All users must agree to our Community Guidelines and Terms of Service.

What topics can I discuss on WhisperChat?

You can discuss a wide range of topics within our community guidelines, including:

  • Entertainment and pop culture
  • Gaming and esports
  • Music and arts
  • Sports and fitness
  • Education and career
  • Lifestyle and wellness
  • General conversation and advice

All conversations must be appropriate, respectful, and follow our guidelines.

How do I report inappropriate behavior?

You can report inappropriate behavior in several ways:

  • During a call: Use the "Report" button in the call interface
  • After a call: Go to your call history and select "Report Issue"
  • Email: Send details to support@whisperchat.com
  • Dashboard: Use the "Report User" feature in your dashboard

All reports are confidential and investigated within 24 hours.

How do I start a call?

To start a call:

  1. Create an account and log in
  2. Browse available Whispers on the homepage
  3. Purchase Whisper Coins (1 coin = 1 call)
  4. Click "Call Now" on a Whisper's profile
  5. You'll be connected for a 5-minute conversation
How much does a call cost?

Each 5-minute call costs 1 Whisper Coin. Coin packages are:

  • $2.99 for 5 coins
  • $4.99 for 10 coins (10% discount)
  • $8.99 for 20 coins (25% discount)
  • $19.99 for 50 coins (33% discount)

All prices are in USD. Coins are non-refundable and expire after 365 days of inactivity.

Can I extend a call beyond 5 minutes?

Currently, all calls are fixed at 5 minutes. However, you can:

  • Start another call with the same Whisper
  • Schedule multiple back-to-back calls (coming soon)
  • Add the Whisper to your favorites for quick access

We're working on longer call durations for future updates.

What if a Whisper doesn't answer my call?

If a Whisper doesn't answer within 30 seconds:

  • The call will automatically end
  • Your coin will be refunded to your account
  • You can try calling another Whisper
  • You can report availability issues through the app

Whispers are required to maintain a high response rate to remain on the platform.

How do I become a Whisper?

To become a Whisper:

  1. Create a regular user account
  2. Go to "Become a Whisper" page
  3. Fill out the application form with your details
  4. Submit required verification documents
  5. Our team reviews applications within 48 hours
  6. Once approved, you can set up your profile and start accepting calls
How much can I earn as a Whisper?

Whispers earn $12 for every 5-minute call. Your potential earnings:

  • 10 calls/day = $120 daily, $600 weekly, $2,400 monthly
  • 20 calls/day = $240 daily, $1,200 weekly, $4,800 monthly
  • 30 calls/day = $360 daily, $1,800 weekly, $7,200 monthly

Earnings are paid bi-weekly via PayPal, bank transfer, or other approved methods.

What are the requirements to be a Whisper?

Basic requirements:

  • 18+ years old with valid ID
  • Clear audio quality (headset recommended)
  • Stable internet connection (10+ Mbps)
  • Working webcam for video calls
  • Quiet, private environment for calls
  • PayPal or bank account for payments
  • Compliance with community guidelines
Can I choose when to receive calls?

Yes! You have full control over your availability:

  • Toggle availability on/off anytime from your dashboard
  • Set your own schedule (work whenever you want)
  • Take breaks between calls as needed
  • Set "Do Not Disturb" hours in your profile

You're an independent contractor, not an employee - you set your own hours.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • Google Pay
  • Apple Pay
  • PayPal
  • Bank transfers (for larger purchases)

All payments are processed securely through Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment processor.

When do Whispers get paid?

Whisper payments are processed:

  • Schedule: Bi-weekly (every 2 weeks)
  • Minimum Payout: $50 (accumulated earnings)
  • Processing Time: 2-3 business days after payout date
  • Methods: PayPal, bank transfer, Wise, or Stripe

You can track your earnings and upcoming payments in your dashboard.

Are there any fees or charges?

Our fee structure:

  • For Callers: No additional fees beyond coin purchases
  • For Whispers: 20% platform fee per call ($2.40 of the $12 rate)
  • Payment Processing: Standard payment processor fees apply
  • Currency Conversion: Market rates + 1% if applicable

All fees are transparent and shown before purchase.

Can I get a refund?

Refund policy:

  • Coins: Generally non-refundable, except in specific cases
  • Technical Issues: Refund if call fails due to platform error
  • Whisper No-show: Automatic coin refund
  • Guideline Violations: Refund if Whisper violates community guidelines
  • Unauthorized Charges: Immediate refund upon verification

Contact support@whisperchat.com for refund requests.

What are the system requirements?

Minimum requirements:

  • Browser: Chrome 80+, Firefox 75+, Safari 13+, Edge 80+
  • Internet: 1+ Mbps upload/download (10+ Mbps recommended)
  • Microphone: Built-in or external (headset recommended)
  • Camera: For video calls (720p or higher recommended)
  • Operating System: Windows 10, macOS 10.13+, Android 8+, iOS 12+
How do I troubleshoot audio/video issues?

Common troubleshooting steps:

  1. Check internet connection speed
  2. Grant browser permissions for microphone/camera
  3. Test with another website to isolate the issue
  4. Try a different browser or device
  5. Restart your router and computer
  6. Update browser to latest version
  7. Check firewall/antivirus settings

If issues persist, contact support with details about your setup.

Is my call recorded?

No, calls are not recorded by default. However:

  • Users can optionally record their own side of calls (must notify other participant)
  • We may temporarily store call metadata (duration, participants, timestamps) for quality and safety purposes
  • We do not store audio or video content without explicit consent
  • All recordings must comply with our guidelines and applicable laws
How do I delete my account?

To delete your account:

  1. Go to your dashboard settings
  2. Click "Account Settings"
  3. Select "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm the deletion

Note: Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Any remaining coins or earnings will be forfeited. Some data may be retained for legal compliance purposes.

# 🔐 FIX FIREBASE AUTHORIZED DOMAINS

## Steps to add your domain:

1. **Go to:** https://console.firebase.google.com/project/house-of-whispers/authentication

2. **Click** "Settings" (gear icon) in the top right

3. **Scroll down** to "Authorized domains"

4. **Add these domains:**
   - `iaiwaf.com`
   - `localhost` (for testing)

5. **Click "Save"**

## Also check Sign-in Methods:
- Ensure "Email/Password" is enabled
- Ensure "Google" is enabled

✅ After adding domains, Google sign-in will work!

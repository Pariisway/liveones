Community Guidelines

Our guidelines ensure a safe, respectful, and enjoyable experience for everyone on WhisperChat.

Respect & Kindness

Treat everyone with respect and kindness

1

Be Respectful

Treat all users with respect, regardless of their background, beliefs, or identity. Everyone deserves a safe and welcoming environment.

  • Use appropriate language and tone
  • Respect differing opinions and perspectives
  • Avoid personal attacks or insults
  • Listen actively and be considerate
2

No Harassment or Bullying

We have zero tolerance for harassment, bullying, or intimidation of any kind.

Prohibited: Threats, stalking, sexual harassment, hate speech, deliberate intimidation, or any behavior that makes others feel unsafe.

3

Protect Privacy

Respect the privacy of others. Do not share personal information without consent.

  • Don't ask for personal contact information
  • Don't share others' personal information
  • Report any privacy violations immediately

Content & Communication

Keep conversations appropriate and meaningful

1

Appropriate Content

All conversations must be appropriate for a general audience. WhisperChat is not a dating platform or adult service.

  • No sexually explicit content or conversations
  • No discussion of illegal activities
  • No graphic violence or disturbing content
  • No promotion of self-harm or dangerous activities
2

No Solicitation

Do not use WhisperChat for commercial solicitation, advertising, or promotion of external services.

Prohibited: Spam, advertising, fundraising, recruitment for external platforms, or any commercial promotion.

3

Quality Conversations

Strive for meaningful, engaging conversations that provide value to both participants.

Encouraged: Sharing experiences, offering advice, discussing interests, providing emotional support, and building genuine connections.

For Whispers

Professional standards for our whisper community

1

Professional Conduct

As a Whisper, you represent our platform. Maintain professional standards at all times.

  • Be punctual for scheduled calls
  • Maintain clear audio/video quality
  • Dress appropriately for video calls
  • Keep your background professional and distraction-free
2

No Guaranteed Outcomes

Do not promise specific outcomes, results, or guaranteed advice. You are providing conversation, not professional services.

Prohibited: Medical advice, financial advice, legal advice, therapy, or any other professional services you are not licensed to provide.

3

Ethical Boundaries

Maintain appropriate boundaries with callers. Report any uncomfortable situations immediately.

  • Do not develop personal relationships with callers
  • Do not share personal contact information
  • End calls that violate guidelines
  • Report inappropriate behavior promptly

Safety & Security

Protecting yourself and others

1

Account Security

Protect your account and personal information.

  • Use a strong, unique password
  • Don't share your account with others
  • Log out from shared devices
  • Enable two-factor authentication if available
2

Reporting System

Use our reporting system to flag violations. All reports are confidential and reviewed promptly.

We investigate all reports within 24 hours. False reports may result in account suspension.

3

Emergency Situations

If you encounter an emergency situation or someone expressing thoughts of self-harm:

  • End the call immediately if you feel unsafe
  • Report the incident to WhisperChat support
  • Encourage the person to contact emergency services
  • Provide crisis resources if appropriate

Violation Consequences

Violations of these guidelines may result in the following consequences, depending on severity:

Warning

First-time minor violations may result in a warning and education about the guidelines.

Temporary Suspension

Repeated or moderate violations may lead to account suspension for 7-30 days.

Permanent Ban

Severe violations or repeated offenses after warnings result in permanent account termination.

Legal Action

Illegal activities will be reported to authorities and may result in legal proceedings.

How to Report Violations

If you encounter behavior that violates our guidelines, please report it immediately:

1

During a Call

Use the "Report" button during the call. The call will be flagged for review.

2

After a Call

Go to your call history and select "Report Issue" next to the call in question.

3

Contact Support

Email support@whisperchat.com with details of the violation.

4

Confidential Review

Our team reviews all reports within 24 hours. We'll notify you of the outcome.

All reports are confidential. We never share reporter information with the reported user.

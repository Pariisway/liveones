Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Whisper Coin?

Each Whisper Coin gives you 5 minutes of conversation with any available whisper. Calls are automatically charged by the minute.

Can I get a refund?

Yes, unused coins can be refunded within 30 days of purchase. Call charges are refundable if the whisper doesn't answer.

Do coins expire?

Coins expire 365 days after purchase. You'll receive email reminders before expiration.

Are there any subscription fees?

No, WhisperChat has no subscription fees. You only pay for the coins you use.
